About KTN Associates

Leading Construction Excellence

KTN Associates is a government registered contractor based in Coimbatore, specializing in comprehensive construction solutions. Under the leadership of Thandavamoorthi V, our Founder & CEO, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the construction industry.

With years of experience and a commitment to quality, we deliver projects that meet the highest standards of construction excellence. Our expertise spans across residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects.

Located in the heart of Coimbatore at Vilankurichi, we serve clients across Tamil Nadu with dedication and professional integrity.
